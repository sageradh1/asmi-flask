About us

AI-Driven tech for scaled video content monetization.

ASMI automates advertising within video content through a proprietary method of analyzing video and instantaneously inserting an ultra-contextual ad.It is the first of its kind technology, and is pivotal to some of the tougher spaces to advertise like short video platforms and streaming services. The manner in which we advertise outperforms other advertising creatives and methods including the popularly-used banners, pre-roll and post-rolls.

The problem ASMI is solving:

Analyzes video content at a very low cost using computer vision.

Uses behavioral pairing algorithm to serve user-specific “contextual” ad.

Dynamically pairs user-specific ads with video content.

Places ad inside video frame where it is least invasive, but still prominent.
